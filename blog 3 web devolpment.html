What is web devlopment ?

Web development is the process of creating, building, and maintaining websites and web applications that are accessible over the internet or an intranet. It involves a combination of programming, design, and content management to produce functional and user-friendly websites.

### Key Aspects of Web

1. **Frontend Development (Client-Side)** - Focuses on the visual and interactive parts of a website that users see and interact with. - Technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, frameworks like React, Angular, Vue.js.

2. **Backend Development (Server-Side)** - Manages the server, databases, and application logic behind the scenes. - Technologies: Programming languages like Python, Java, PHP, Ruby, Node.js; databases like MySQL, MongoDB, PostgreSQL.

3. **Full Stack Development** - Combines both frontend and backend development skills.

4. **Web Design** - Involves creating the layout, user interface, and overall aesthetic of the website.

5. **Web Hosting and Deployment** - Publishing the website on servers so that it is accessible via the internet.

### Purpose of Web Development: - To create websites for various purposes such as personal blogs, business portals, e-commerce stores, social networks, and more. - To provide users with seamless, engaging, and functional online experiences.

### In summary: Web development is the craft of building websites and web applications, involving coding, design, and technical skills to bring digital ideas to life on the internet.
